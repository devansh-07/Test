The Samsung Galaxy A73 has a powerful octa-core processor with a clock speed of 2.4GHz and 1.8GHz. It has a large, vibrant 6.7-inch Super AMOLED Plus display with a resolution of 1080 x 2400 and a color depth of 16 million. The rear camera of the device has a resolution of 108.0 MP + 12.0 MP + 5.0 MP + 5.0 MP. It has multiple f-numbers (F1.8, F2.2, F2.4, F2.4) and comes with auto focus and optical image stabilization (OIS) features. It also has a digital zoom of up to 10x. The front camera has a resolution of 32.0 MP and an f-number of F2.2, however, it doesn't have auto focus or OIS. Both rear and front cameras come with a flash. The device is capable of recording videos in UHD 4K (3840 x 2160) with a framerate of 30fps and slow-motion videos at 240fps in HD. The memory of the device has a RAM size of 8 GB and a ROM size of 128 GB, with 99.6 GB of available memory. It also supports MicroSD cards up to 1TB. The device has dual SIM support with nano-SIM card slots, one of which is a hybrid slot that can be used for a second SIM or a MicroSD card. This device is a dual-SIM device, with a Nano-SIM (4FF) slot type of SIM 1 + Hybrid (SIM or MicroSD). It supports 2G GSM (GSM850, GSM900, DCS1800, PCS1900), 3G WCDMA (B1(2100), B2(1900), B4(AWS), B5(850), B8(900)), 4G LTE FDD (B1(2100), B2(1900), B3(1800), B4(AWS), B5(850), B7(2600), B8(900), B12(700), B13(700), B17(700), B20(800), B26(850), B28(700), B66(AWS-3)), 4G LTE TDD (B38(2600), B40(2300), B41(2500)), 5G Sub6 FDD (N1(2100), N3(1800), N5(850), N7(2600), N28(700), N66(AWS-3)), and 5G Sub6 TDD (N40(2300), N41(2500), N78(3500)). It has GPS, Glonass, Beidou, Galileo, and QZSS location technologies and uses USB Type-C with USB 2.0. Wi-Fi connectivity is supported with 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax, 2.4G+5GHz, and Bluetooth 5.0. The device also has NFC support. The device has the following sensors: Accelerometer, Fingerprint Sensor, Gyro Sensor, Geomagnetic Sensor, Light Sensor, and Proximity Sensor. The device's dimensions are 163.7mm in height, 76.1mm in width, and 7.6mm in depth. The device weighs 181g. The device's battery has a typical capacity of 5000mAh and is not removable. The device's internet usage time on LTE is up to 18 hours, internet usage time on Wi-Fi is up to 17 hours, video playback time wirelessly is up to 21 hours, audio playback time wirelessly is up to 85 hours, and the talk time on 4G LTE is up to 45 hours. The audio and video capabilities of the device include stereo support and the ability to play video in the format MP4, M4V, 3GP, 3G2, AVI, FLV, MKV, WEBM at a resolution of UHD 4K (3840 x 2160)@30fps. For audio, it can play MP3, M4A, 3GA, AAC, OGG, OGA, WAV, AMR, AWB, FLAC, MID, MIDI, XMF, MXMF, IMY, RTTTL, RTX, OTA.
